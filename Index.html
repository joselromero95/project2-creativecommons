What We Do
Creative Commons is a nonprofit organization that helps
overcome legal obstacles to the sharing of knowledge
and creativity to address the world’s pressing challenges.

Share Your Work

Use Creative Commons tools to help share your work. Our free,
easy-to-use copyright licenses provide a simple, standardized way
to give your permission to share and use your creative work— on
conditions of your choice. You can adopt one of our licenses by
sharing on a platform, or choosing a license.
